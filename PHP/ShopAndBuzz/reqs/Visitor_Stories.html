Visitors:

Visitor Role is used by users that have not logged into the site.

Visitors must register for the site.

Registration requires a unique username, an appropriate password and a unique email address. Appropriate password means at least 8 characters with 2 numeric.
http://buzz.alliances.org/signup/

Visitors become BuzzUsers when they log in.

Password is validated. User gets a session cookie.
http://buzz.alliances.org/login/

Visitors can view static HTML pages related to the Buzz Service and Concept.

Content will be provided and maintained by Admin Users.
http://buzz.alliances.org/useragreement/
http://buzz.alliances.org/customerservice/
http://buzz.alliances.org/privacypolicy/
http://buzz.alliances.org/about/

Visitors can search the Buzz database for Sellers.

Search can be performed based on category, Seller email address or Seller name.
http://buzz.alliances.org/sellers/

If a Visitor fails to login properly, they are shown a message stating that, and provided a link to recover username password or both.

http://buzz.alliances.org/forgot/
