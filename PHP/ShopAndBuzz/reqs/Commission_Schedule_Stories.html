Commission Schedule:

Commission Schedule related to the commissions paid by Sellers to Buyers who recommend them when purchases are made based on recommendations.

Sellers have the ability to set schedules for two levels of commissions.

First level and Second level.

Buyers who recommend Sellers comprise First Level commisions.

BuzzUsers who make purchases from a Seller for whom they have a referral comprise Second Level commisions.

Commisions are transferred from Seller's Paypal account to the recipients' Paypal accounts.

If Seller has insufficient funds in his Paypal account, the Seller is deactivated.

Commissions earned will not be paid out until the UPI window has passed for the sale generating the commission and no UPI being filed on the sale.

Schedules can be made up of several components:

  • Flat fee per sale
  • Percentage per sale
  • % off purchase
  • flat amount off purchase exceeding threshhold.
  • Duration
  • Schedule terminates after XX purchases
  • Commission for purchases from previous Buyers?
  • One time credit
