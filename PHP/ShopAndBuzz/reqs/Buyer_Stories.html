Buyer:

Buyer Role is used when recommending a Seller and when calculating commissions.

Buyers will be able to recommend Sellers that they have done business with.

The set of available Sellers that the Buyer can recommend include the set of Sellers that are Sellers-in-good-standing at eBay and with whom the Buyers have completed a sale. At least one sale between Buyer and Seller must be beyond the UPI window and not be under UPI.

Buyers will be required to provide the following information to refer a Seller:

  • Number of times you bought from Seller
  • Rate the Seller (1-10) in the following categories:
    • Speed of transaction
    • How Item met expectation
    • Value (for what you paid)
    • Seller communication
    • Overall experience
  • Why do you recommend the Seller (500 Characters)

Buyers can only recommand a Seller once.

Buyer's recommendation of a Seller will be sent in an email to the Buyer's Hive.

The email is delivered to the Hive but also taking into account the email delivery preferences of the recipients.

A Buyer's recommendations will be grouped together into a section of the Buyer's public profile called "My Honeycomb".

Buyer's recommendation information will be available on the Seller's profile page.

Buyers can run a report showing sales from their recommended Sellers and their commissions received and pending.

Buyers who recommend a Seller have the opportunity to earn a commission.

Sellers can configure their account to set commissions algorithms

Buyers who buy from a recommended Seller have the opportunity to earn a Second level commission.

  • Sellers can configure their account to set second level commission algorithms.
  • Second level commissions will only be offered for Buyers connected to the Seller through another Buyer when that relationship was eestablished prior to the sale.
  • Second level commissions may be offered to Buyers who have a history with the Seller. This is up to the Seller's discretion.

Buyers' commissions accrue over a calendar month and paid out on the 5th of the month.

This was stated in the requirements, but may need to be modified to accomodate the possibility that a UPI is entered against the sale.

If a Buyer wins an auction but does not pay, the system will not issue the commission.

  • The eBay API will issue a UPI on a transaction in that case.
  • The UPI may not appear in the system for a significant amount of time.
