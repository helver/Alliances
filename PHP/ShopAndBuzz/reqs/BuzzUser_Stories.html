BuzzUser:

BuzzUser Role is used by default after the BuzzUser has been authenticated.

BuzzUser's registration must be confirmed.

  • Once the BuzzUser's account is created, an email is sent to the email address used to create the account.
  • The registration is confirmed once the Visitor follows the link contained in the email and enters the code provided in the email.
http://buzz.alliances.org/signup/confirmemail/

BuzzUsers can recover their username and password using a "forgot password" feature.

  • No visual cues about the existance or non-existance of an account associated with the email address will provided to the user when the this feature is engaged.
  • Username and password (but only one at a time) will be sent to the BuzzUser's email address.
  • If no account exists for the email address supplied, the email will contain a message stating that and a link to the registration page.
    http://buzz.alliances.org/forgot/

    BuzzUsers can choose a "remember me" option that will log them in automatically.

    • Special cookie stored for longer period of time.
    • Not a specific session cookie, but a user hint.
    • Editing account settings, relationships or delivering email will require authentication (like LinkedIn).
    http://buzz.alliances.org/login/

    BuzzUsers can configure how often to receive email notifications from the site.

    • Options include: Immediately, Daily, Weekly or Never.
    • BuzzUsers can also elect to not receive emails from specific BuzzUsers.
    • Emails include:
      • Seller additions
      • Referrals
    http://buzz.alliances.org/users/testypoo/preferences/

    BuzzUsers will have a public profile page.

    • The public profile page will contain Seller information if the BuzzUser is a registered Seller.
    • The public profile page will contain Hive information if the BuzzUser has a Hive.
    • Additionally, Hive information will be displayed (new recommended Sellers, etc).
    • The BuzzUser's public profile page URL will be http://<domain>/username/.
    • There will be several domain names that will all function in a similar fashion.
    http://buzz.alliances.org/users/testypoo/

    Every BuzzUser has a private profile page.

    • Contains:
      • Interface for searching for Sellers
      • How many users are in My Hive and a list of them.
      • List of new Sellers added to the Honeycombs of my Hive.
      • Total commisions earned and paid by Seller and aggregated.
      • System announcements (Seller inactive, Hive adds, payments, site feature)
      • Links to public profile, accounts page, edit account info.
    http://buzz.alliances.org/users/testypoo/private/

    BuzzUsers will be checked up on daily.

    If the BuzzUser falls out of good standing with eBay, the BuzzUsers's account is inactivated.
    http://buzz.alliances.org/users/testypoo/private/

    BuzzUsers can invite people to the site.

    • Invitees automatically have accounts created for them in with their email address.
    • Referrals are emailed to the prospective users.
    • Referrals will contain two links. First link takes the prospective user to the registration page where they complete their registration and are automatically included in the Hive of the BuzzUser that referred them.
    • Second link takes the propective user to an email notification configuration page that allows the user to configure how often they will receive email from the site (just like a normal < a href="Glossary_BuzzUser.html">BuzzUser).
    http://buzz.alliances.org/nomoreemail/myemail/
    http://buzz.alliances.org/inviteothers/

    BuzzUsers can recommend that a Seller register.

    Invitiation is an email sent to the prospective Seller. This would be an email inviting a Seller to register as a Seller. This is different from a normal invitation only in the text of the email that is delivered.
    http://buzz.alliances.org/inviteothers/

    BuzzUsers will be able to create a network of friends.

    • The network will be the universe of BuzzUsers.
    • The network is called "My Hive".
    • Hive additions are unidirectional.
    • When I add someone to "My Hive", the victim is given the opportunity to add me to theirs.
    • Relationship: BU | Victim | Hive | Honeycomb | Referred By | Commission Sched
    http://buzz.alliances.org/users/testypoo/

    A BuzzUser's referrals do not expire.

    This may be changed in the future.

    BuzzUsers can remove themselves from another BuzzUser's Hive.

    This removal is silent and permanent.
    http://buzz.alliances.org/users/testypoo/blacklist/

    Any time a BuzzUser hits a link back to eBay, the AsWas affiliate code will be included in the link.

    When a BuzzUser does something that would trigger a referral and the BuzzUser already has a referral for the Seller, no new referral is created.

    The first referral for a Seller wins regardless of how the referral was created.

    A BuzzUser may get a referral for a Seller when the BuzzUser views a Buyer's "My Honeycomb" page.

    In this case, the Buyer is the recommender.
    http://buzz.alliances.org/users/testypoo/honeycomb/

    A BuzzUser may get a referral when a Buyer creates a recommendation for a Seller.

    This would only happen if the system actually attempts to deliver the email to the BuzzUser. In other words, nothing in the Buzz system (configurations, blacklists, email queueing, etc) would have stopped the email from being delivered.

    A BuzzUser may get a referral when the Visitor adds the Seller directly - by doing a category search for example.

    In this case, there is no recommender.
    http://buzz.alliances.org/sellers/

    BuzzUsers who are made inactive:
    • Will not receive emails from the system
    • Will receive an email informing them that they have been made inactive
    • Will have a default template displayed instead of their profile pages
    • Will be removed from any Hives/Honeycombs

    BuzzUsers who are re-activated will have their accounts return to their former state.

    BuzzUsers are not required to provide their PayPal information.

    Unless Paypal information is provided, no commissions can be paid.
