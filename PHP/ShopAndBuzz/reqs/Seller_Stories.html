Seller:

The Seller Role is required when being recommended by a Buyer or when registering as a Buzz Seller.

Sellers must register with the site to be a Seller.

  • Sellers must provide their:
    • eBay User ID
    • approve "Auth & Auth"
    • PayPal email address
    • approve Paypal token.
    • Seller category
    • Description of their business

Sellers must have meet a minimum set of criteria in order to register as a Seller

  • Seller must have an eBay feedback score of 100 or higher.
  • Seller must have 98% or higher positive comments.

Sellers will be charged a monthly Buzz fee.

  • Fee will be calculated according to the Fee Schedule.
  • Fee will be taken from the Seller's Paypal account.

Sellers will be inactivate if they don't have enough cash in their Paypal account to cover commisions earned by Buzz Users.

Seller's profile page will contain links to Buyers' recommendations.

Seller's profile page will contain average ratings from Buyers.

Seller's profile page will contain eBay Seller information.

  • Feedback number.
  • Percentage positive
  • eBay PowerSeller?

Sellers can run reports in their account area.

  • How many recommendations - who are Buyers?
  • How many sales are from Buzz users?
  • Month-by-Month reports for sales and commissions paid.
  • Canned reports by request.
